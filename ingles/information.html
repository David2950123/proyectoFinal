Tips to reduce our water consumption

Our water consumption can vary depending on the task we have to perform, reduce water consumption and the time we use it, this would help our planet and our expenses.

We make a list of tips that you could take into account to reduce your consumption.

  • Turn off the tap while you shave or brush your teeth.
  • Prevent taps from dripping when you're not using them.
  • Wash fruits and vegetables in a container and not under the running tap. To defrost food you can do the same.
  • Use the water from the previous point to water the plants.
  • Check the toilet tank for water leaks.
  • It collects the rainwater to be able to water the plants or the garden.
  • It is better to bathe pets in the shower than with a hose, due to the decrease in the amount of water.
  • In each shower, close the tap when you are lathering.
